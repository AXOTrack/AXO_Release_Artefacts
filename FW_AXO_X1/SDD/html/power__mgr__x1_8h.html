Detailed Description
The tasks of the power manager X1 are:
  • Controlling the boost manager
  • Controlling the metrics transmission interval
  • Reacting on ADXL wakeup because of activity threshold exceeding Because data transmission via LTE needs more capacity than the battery can provide, the boost converter needs to be enabled to load the supercap Capacitor. After 30 seconds (determined experimentally) the load is enough to ensure the complete transmission of metrics or measurement data and the boost converter can be disabled. A timer of the power manager control the boost on timeout.
    Another timer of the power manager controls the metrics transmission interval. On expiry the boost converter will be enabled, metrics will be collected and transmission starts after 30 seconds.
    Also on ADXL wakeup the boost converter will be enabled and the measurement data will be send after 30 seconds.
