Detailed Description
Measurement raw data will be stored in FRAM. FRAM space needs to be allocated and reserved. Every second acceleration data will be measured and stored with a data rate of 4400 samples per second. Maximal 15 times acceleration data shall be measured, that means after 15 seconds the complete measurement cycle is finished and the RMS data will be send via LTE.
